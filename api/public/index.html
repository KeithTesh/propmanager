For Property Management Agencies

Your landlords trust you
with their investments

PropManager gives you the tools to manage their portfolios professionally — and the transparency to prove it.

📊
Landlord Portal Access
Invite your landlord clients to their own read-only portal. They see occupancy rates, rent collected, and full remittance history — no more calls asking "how much did you collect?"
💰
Transparent Remittance Statements
Generate monthly statements showing exactly: Gross collected → minus your commission → minus approved expenses = Net payable. PDF export for every landlord every month.

Dispute Resolution Built In
Landlords can flag a statement that looks wrong with one click. You respond in the system, they see your response. All history preserved. Trust issues resolved before they become phone arguments.

Flexible Commission Models
Set a percentage of rent collected or a flat monthly fee — per landlord or per property. Commission on partial payments calculated on what was actually collected, not what was billed.

Agent Notes on Statements
Explain deductions, repairs and delays in plain language. Notes are visible to landlords in their portal. Good agents write notes. Great agents use PropManager to show them.

Manage Multiple Landlords
One dashboard for all your landlord clients. See the full portfolio at a glance — total units, occupancy, collections, pending remittances. Each landlord sees only their own data.

Frequently asked questions

How long is the free trial?▼
Your free trial lasts 7 days from signup. No credit card required. Full access to all Growth plan features.
How does M-Pesa integration work?▼
PropManager connects to your M-Pesa PayBill. Payments are automatically matched to the correct bill using the account reference. You can also send STK push requests directly to tenants.
How many SMS do I get?▼
Starter plans include 500 SMS per month. Growth plans include 1,500 SMS. Enterprise is unlimited. Request a quota increase anytime from settings.
Can I manage multiple properties?▼
Yes. Add unlimited properties and manage them from one dashboard. Each has its own units, tenants, leases and bills.
Is my data safe?▼
Yes. Bank-grade security. Encrypted servers, SSL/TLS connections, and complete company data isolation.
What payment methods do tenants use?▼
M-Pesa PayBill, M-Pesa Till, or bank transfer. Automatically reconciled. Cash payments can be recorded manually.
Can I cancel anytime?▼
Yes. No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime and your account remains active until the end of the billing period.
